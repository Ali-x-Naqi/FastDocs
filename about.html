About FastDocs

FastDocs is a dedicated platform designed to centralize and provide easy access to academic resources for the students of FAST CFD. Our goal is to streamline the process of finding course materials like notes, past papers, slides, and assignments, making your academic journey smoother and more efficient.

Our Mission

We aim to empower students by creating a comprehensive and well-organized digital library of course materials. We believe that easy access to resources is key to success and we are committed to building a platform that supports collaborative learning within the university community.

Who We Are

This project was created by Ali Naqi as an open-source initiative. It is a testament to the power of a community-driven approach to education, where students can contribute and benefit from a shared repository of knowledge.
